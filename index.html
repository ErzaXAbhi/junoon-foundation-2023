We Are

Junoon Foundation

A leading non-profit organization based in Palghar, India, dedicated to transforming lives through its impactful work in the education sector. Since its establishment in April 2013, Junoon Foundation has made a profound difference in numerous lives, leveraging its expertise and passion for education.
