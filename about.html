Knowing Junoon Foundation

Vision:

Junoon strives to foster an equitable partnership characterized by strength, sustainability, and dignity between urban centers and rural communities. Our mission is to leverage underutilized resources as catalysts for dignified development. We aspire to expand our concept globally, transcending geographical boundaries and economic disparities. We harness surplus urban materials to tackle fundamental yet overlooked challenges faced by financially disadvantaged individuals. Our approach empowers them to take an active role in crafting and implementing their solutions, drawing upon their own expertise and preserving their dignity, with materials provided as a token of appreciation for their involvement.

Mission:

Education: To impart life skills and soft skills, provide career guidance and encourage the love for arts and sports among children.

Social Awareness: To spread awareness regarding education, health, hygiene, government policies, social responsibilities, tolerance and unity.

Sustainable Development: Junoon is trying to preserve the fast depleting cover of trees and vanishing jungles. We organise tree plantation drives, and hold rallies to create awerness among citizens about the importance of nature and sustainable agriculture.
